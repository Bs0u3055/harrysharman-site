Hard rules

The boring rules are the business.

The experiment only works if the agent is forced to prove things. Otherwise it becomes a very elegant machine for producing confident nonsense.

No spend The GBP 100 reserve stays locked until verified non-Harry revenue exists or Harry explicitly overrides it.
No fake proof No revenue, subscribers, reviews, sends, dashboard status or sales claims without proof.
No secrets No credentials, private logs, personal account details or platform evasion instructions.
No cold drift Buyer intent beats speculative outreach. If people want the work, we look for raised hands first.
