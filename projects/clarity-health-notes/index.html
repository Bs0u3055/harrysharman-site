Clear boundaries matter.

These trackers are designed for self-observation, appointment preparation and client-provider conversation. They do not diagnose, treat, prevent or promise health outcomes. Provider approval is required before a custom version is shared with clients.

  • No claims that tracking will improve, treat or manage a condition.
  • No replacement for medical advice, clinical assessment or emergency care.
  • Condition wording can be adapted to your professional standards.
  • Plain-language disclaimers are included in every pack.
