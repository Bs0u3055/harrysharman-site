About the show

AI news, without the noise.

Briefly, AI is an experiment in fully automated media. Every morning, a pipeline pulls the day's most interesting AI stories, writes a script, runs it through a voice model trained on Harry's voice, and publishes — without anyone pressing a button.

The result is a tight 5–7 minute briefing on what actually matters in AI that day. No ads. No filler. No human gatekeeping.
